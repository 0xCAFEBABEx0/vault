## Abstract / Summary
NVIDIA released Nemotron 3.5 Content Safety, a compact 4B-parameter multimodal and multilingual safety model for enterprise AI guardrails. It evaluates user prompts, optional images, and optional assistant responses as a unified context, supports custom organization-specific policies, and can optionally emit auditable THINK-mode reasoning traces. The release also includes a multimodal multilingual safety dataset and targets real-time deployment through transformers, vLLM, SGLang, NVIDIA NIM, and third-party inference providers.
