## Overview
A comprehensive benchmark suite that evaluates frontier LLMs and agentic frameworks across the full research lifecycle, from literature review to paper writing.

## Key Contributions
- Multi-stage benchmark covering: literature discovery, hypothesis generation, experimental design, code implementation, result analysis, and paper writing
- Evaluation of both standalone LLMs and agentic harnesses
- Comparison with human researchers on realistic research tasks

## Benchmark Design
- Literature Review: assessing ability to find relevant papers and synthesize knowledge
- Hypothesis Generation: evaluating novelty and feasibility of proposed research directions
- Experimental Design: testing ability to design rigorous experiments
- Implementation: coding research methods and running experiments
- Analysis: interpreting results and drawing valid conclusions
- Writing: producing publication-quality manuscripts

## Results
- Frontier LLMs show strong performance on literature review and writing tasks
- Agentic harnesses significantly improve implementation and analysis quality
- Gap remains between AI and expert human researchers in hypothesis generation

## Significance
Provides a rigorous, end-to-end evaluation of AI capabilities in research, revealing both strengths and current limitations of LLM-based research assistants.
