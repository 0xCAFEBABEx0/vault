## Abstract
Controlled study of provenance-grounded filtering and adaptive recovery in synthetic post-training pipelines. Exact source provenance improves faithfulness gating; hallucination and reward gates reject disjoint populations; adaptive recovery achieves higher yield than naive resampling.
