## Abstract
Cross-lingual distributional skew in frontier LLMs tested via geopolitical wargame. Llama-4 shows increased coercive rhetoric under Turkish; Gemini-3.1-Pro and DeepSeek-R1 show decreases. Skew is architecture-dependent, not universal.
