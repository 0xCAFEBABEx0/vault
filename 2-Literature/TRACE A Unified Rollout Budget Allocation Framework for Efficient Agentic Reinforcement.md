## Abstract
TRACE is a unified rollout allocation framework for multi-turn agentic RL. Models each ReAct turn as a distinct node, allocating budget to prompt roots and intermediate prefixes most likely to yield mixed terminal rewards. Improves Qwen3-14B Multi-Hop QA accuracy by 2.8 points over baselines at equal sampling cost.
